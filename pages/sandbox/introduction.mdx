# Introduction


## About Epicode

Epicode provides enterprise-grade telephony infrastructure and middleware for scalable voice AI deployments. Built for modern communication workflows, the platform enables seamless integration across telephony networks, voice channels, and AI voicebot frameworks.

Designed with an API-first architecture, Epicode supports high-scale outbound and inbound calling use cases while offering deployment flexibility across cloud, hybrid, and on-premise environments.

---

## Core Capabilities

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 1. Multi-Channel Voice Connectivity With IraVoice </span>

Enable communication across PSTN, SIP, WebRTC, and WhatsApp channels with unified telephony integration for voicebots and AI-driven calling workflows.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 2. Voice AI Platform Integration  </span>

Integrate seamlessly with platforms such as LiveKit, Vapi, and other voice AI frameworks, supporting proxy-mode call handling and external AI orchestration.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 3. Proven High-Scale Performance </span>

Optimized to handle **5M+ daily calls** with efficient resource utilization, supporting up to **1000 concurrent calls on an 8-core VM**.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 4. Dedicated Infrastructure </span>

Deploy with dedicated VMs, bandwidth allocation, DID numbers, and configurable CPS (Calls Per Second) limits tailored to deployment requirements.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 5. Flexible Deployment Models </span>

Support for cloud, hybrid, and on-premise deployments to align with enterprise infrastructure, compliance, and operational requirements.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 6. Fast & Accurate CPA Detection </span>

Outbound CPA (Call Progress Analysis) detection in as little as **1.75 seconds** with **95%–98% accuracy** for efficient outbound campaign automation.

### <span style={{"color":"#b85cfa"}}> 7. Enterprise-Grade Scalability & Reliability </span>

A hyper-scalable telephony stack engineered for **99.99% uptime**, ensuring reliable communication for mission-critical voice AI applications.
